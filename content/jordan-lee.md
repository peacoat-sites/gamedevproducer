Jordan Lee went from solo developer to small studio founder, shipping three commercial titles across PC and mobile over six years. Along the way he learned most of what he knows about pricing, discoverability, and platform strategy the hard way — by getting them wrong first.

At GameDevProducer, Jordan covers the business side of indie development: Steam algorithms, pricing strategy, how to build a sustainable studio without outside funding, and what actually moves the needle on launch day.
