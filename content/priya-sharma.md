Priya Sharma is a game designer with a focus on systems design and interactive narrative. She has contributed to projects ranging from tabletop adaptations to mobile narrative games, and holds a graduate degree in human-computer interaction with a specialization in game UX.

At GameDevProducer, Priya brings a design-first lens to the craft of building engaging games — covering mechanics, player psychology, level design, progression systems, and how to tell a story through the way a game feels to play.
