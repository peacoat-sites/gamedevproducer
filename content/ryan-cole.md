Ryan Cole has spent over eight years working in game production — first as a project coordinator at a mid-size studio, then as a freelance producer for indie teams across PC and console. He specializes in milestone planning, GDD structure, and keeping development on schedule without burning out the team.

At GameDevProducer, Ryan covers the practical side of production: the systems, tools, and workflows that help games actually ship.
