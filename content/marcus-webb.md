Marcus Webb is a software engineer turned game developer with a background in systems programming and real-time graphics. He's shipped projects in Unity, Unreal Engine 5, and Godot, and spent several years as a technical lead at a mid-size studio focused on multiplayer PC games.

At GameDevProducer, Marcus writes technical deep-dives: engine comparisons, performance optimization, build pipeline setup, and the technical decisions that shape how a game gets built.
