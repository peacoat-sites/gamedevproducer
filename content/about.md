## Why this site exists

After years of producing games across multiple studios and platforms, I kept writing down the same answers to the same questions — for new producers joining the team, for peers prepping for milestone reviews, for anyone trying to figure out if Scrum actually works in a game dev context (spoiler: it depends).

This site is those notes, made public and kept up to date.

## Who writes it

**Stephen Brenish** is a Game Producer with experience shipping titles across console, PC, and mobile platforms. The best-practice articles are written from direct production experience. The news and data sections aggregate publicly available industry sources.

*This site is a personal project. Views expressed are my own and do not represent any employer.*

## What you'll find here

- **Best practices** — frameworks and templates I've actually used in production
- **Tool guides** — honest takes on JIRA, Confluence, Notion, Hacknplan, and others in a game dev context
- **Career guides** — for aspiring producers and those leveling up from associate to senior to executive
- **Industry news** — aggregated daily from Game Developer, GDC, and major gaming outlets
- **Steam data** — new releases and trend tracking, updated daily
