Game production is about knowing what matters and what doesn't. Stephen Brenish spent over a decade shipping indie games, learning the business side from the ground up: Steam algorithms, publisher relationships, audience building, and the thousand small decisions that determine success or failure.

GameDevProducer exists because the advice Stephen needed when starting out was scattered across forums, podcasts, and conversations with other developers. This site consolidates that hard-won knowledge into a resource for developers who want to understand the business side of making games.
