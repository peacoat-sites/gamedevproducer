Samantha Roberts has worked on both sides of the publisher relationship: first pitching games at conferences as a developer, then as a consultant helping studios prepare for funding rounds and platform deals. She's sat across the table from publishers at PAX, GDC, and in countless video calls.

At GameDevProducer, Samantha covers the business of getting your game out there: how to build a pitch deck that lands, what developers should know before signing, and how to navigate the modern publishing landscape.
