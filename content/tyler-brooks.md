Tyler Brooks spent a decade managing teams at both AAA and indie studios before transitioning to consulting. He's helped studios through crunch, layoffs, restructuring, and the transition from small team to full studio. He has strong opinions about all of it.

At GameDevProducer, Tyler writes about the human side of game development: how to hire well, build a culture that produces good work, navigate conflict on a small team, and run a studio that people actually want to stay at.
